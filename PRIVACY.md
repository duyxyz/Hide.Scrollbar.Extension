# Privacy Policy for Hide Scrollbar Extension

**Last Updated: April 11, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Hide Scrollbar" extension ("we", "us", or "our") handles user data.

## 1. Data Collection
The "Hide Scrollbar" extension does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data to external servers.

## 2. Permissions Usage
- **Storage**: Used locally on your device to save your preferences (on/off state) and your website whitelist.
- **Tabs/Scripting**: Used solely to detect when a page is loaded and inject the CSS required to hide the scrollbar.
- **Host Permissions (<all_urls>)**: Necessary to allow the extension to hide scrollbars on all websites you visit as per its core functionality.

## 3. Data Sharing
Since we do not collect any data, no data is shared with third parties.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions, please contact us via the extension's support channel.
